ABOUT WIKTOR.

Wiktor Kozłowski, Ph.D. is a doctor of biological sciences, osteologist, and long-time scientist at the Mammal Research Institute of the Polish Academy of Sciences (MRI-PAS) in Białowieża.

Kozłowski was born on April 20, 1935, in Gdańsk to Karol and Zofia (née Matecka) Ruprecht.

Kozłowski passed away on July 2, 2010, in Bydgoszcz and was buried on July 10, 2010, at the Municipal Cemetery in Włocławek. He is survived by his children, Dr. Zofia Ruprecht and Dr. Lech Ruprecht, and grandchildren, Victoria and Michał Ruprecht.
